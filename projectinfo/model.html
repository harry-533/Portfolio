Chess Engine

A website made to compare and use labeling ML models to help with a research project at my university

Project Description

During my second year I reached out to my teachers to get involved in research projects at my university. I was tasked with finding a free and easy to use alternative to Google Vision for image labeling. This website shows a comparison between the model I found and trained and Google Vision. It also has a feature to take a folder of images and return an excel spreadsheet of labels.
