About Me

Throughout highschool and college programming and computing interested me but i focused my time on graphic design planning to do this at university, but after a gap year I switched to computer science as the innovative tech industry was much more appealing than digital design.

I ended my first year of computer science at the University of Huddersfield with a 92% grade, and with second year starting I aim to continue working hard to keep getting good grades through the rest of university as I hope to complete a masters degree at a top university.

Most of my time outside of computer science is dedicated to my love of playing and watching sports including football where i am a Manchester United fan, Formula 1 where i support Mclaren and American football where i support the KC Chiefs.

When i complete my undergraduate degree I hope that thanks to hardwork I can continue my studies at masters level and maybe a PHD as I am very passionate about computer science and want to keep improving so that I can in the longterm have a long impactful career in the tech industry. I enjoy studying all aspects of computing but two sectors of tech that I plan to learn more about are artificial intelligence and computational finance
